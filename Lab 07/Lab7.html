The Moon Festival is one of the most significant Chinese festivals in the Chinese community. This year, the Moon Festival falls on October 4th. The Moon Festival symbolizes the reunion of family and sweet memories. During the Moon Festival, people gather to share joy and bless one another for good fortune in the coming year, all the while enjoying mooncakes. Mooncakes are an essential part of the festival celebration. Every year, mooncakes are the best gift for your families and friends to celebrate the Moon Festival.
