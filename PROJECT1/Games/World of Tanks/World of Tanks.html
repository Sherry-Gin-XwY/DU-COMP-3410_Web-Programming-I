World of Tanks

World of Tnks is a team-based massively multiplayer online game dedicated to armored warfare in the mid-20century.

Throw yourself into epic tank battles shoulder to shoulder with other steel cowboys to domiunate the world with tank supremacy!

Light Tanks

Light Tanks are small, agile tank which often take the roles of flankers or scout.

They are represented by a solid green or red diamond on the mini-map and over tehir respective target marker.



Medium Tanks

Medium tanks are multi-purpose tanks with balanced characteristic that can assume many different roles in game.

They are represented by a two-piece green or red diamond on the mini map and over their respective target marker.



Heavy Tanks

Heavy Tanks are large, heavy vehicles which often act as a team's vanguard.

They are represneted by a three-piece green or red diamond on the mini-map and over their resprective target marker.



Tank Destroyer

Tank destoryers(also know as TDs for short) are specialized armored fighting vehicles designed specifically for destroying heavily armored tanks.

They are represented by a green or red inverted traiangle on the mini-mao and over their repective target marker.



Self-Propelled Guns

Self-propelled Guns(also known as artilery, SPGs, arty) are fragile armored vehicles which provide indirect fire support from a long distance.

They are represented by a green or red square in the mini-mip and over their respective target marker.
